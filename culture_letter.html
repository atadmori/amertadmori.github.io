Culture Letter ~ Amer Tadmori

I'm applying for the Business Intelligence & Revenue Analyst Role!

I'm currently finishing up my Master's in Applied Statistics at Colorado State University (graduating in May) and previously worked in Rental Housing Analytics at TransUnion. I would be delighted to join the team at InvitedHome and hit the ground running. I bring a great blend of statistical chops, technological aptitude (Excel, R, SQL, Tableau), and business sense to help InvitedHome stay ahead of the competition. While I don't have a vacation home myself, I have stayed in several luxury vacation rentals - most recently in Napa Valley (though admittedly it was booked through a competitor...).

During my time with TransUnion Rental Screening, I worked with multi-family and single-family property management companies to forecast eviction rates and help customers with onboarding onto TransUnion's screening platform. I also built and automated reporting solutions for our customers so they could get to the insights they needed to take action. As part of the leadership team, I was also responsible for the strategic vision of the group, so my work was essential in forming the business team's 3-year plan. While at Nielsen, I was also working in analytics - there I used statistical analysis to understand past trends and forecast the effect of marketing tactics on sales for large CPG clients and brands.

Prior to that, I was at iModerate, a small market research company where I truly lived the credo of "not my job is not in the vocabulary". My official role there was Project Director but my work included moderating conversations, writing reports, mentoring junior contributors, and planning potlucks.

In between my work I've managed to live a key InvitedHome core value: “Vacations are essential to the soul”. I've traveled the US and beyond (check the Choose Destination button!). My most memorable vacations so far have been climbing Dufourspitze in Switzerland and descending down the world's highest via ferrata on Mt. Kinabalu in Malaysia.

Thanks for reading and thanks for the consideration! I'd love to connect to learn more about InvitedHome and how I can help drive growth!
