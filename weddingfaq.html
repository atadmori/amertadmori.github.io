Where do you recommend we eat in Denver?

Here are some of our favorites:

  • Denver Biscuit Company - Giant breakfast sandwiches served on homemade biscuits
  • Park Burger - Sit down burgers (although Denver also has a Shake Shack now...)
  • Pho 95 - consistently one of the top Pho places in Denver
  • Taste of Thailand - typical Thai dishes like Pad Thai as well as some more traditional ones
  • Sushi Den - Best sushi in Denver but a bit spendy
  • Taqueria Patzcuaro - Small and pretty authentic neighborhood place. Great Torta sandwiches.
  • La Loma - The best fajitas (with homemade tortillas).
  • El Five - Spanish and mediterranean tapas. Amazing paella and amazing view.
  • Sweet Cow - A couple locations in Denver. Our favorite!! (there is also a Voodoo Donuts in Denver if you don't like ice cream although who doesn't like ice cream?)
